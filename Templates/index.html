Description:

UBS is a Swiss global financial services company. The company provides wealth management, asset management, and investment banking services for private, corporate, and institutional clients worldwide. ZEUS is a central database of UBS which provides single view of Wealth Management credit risk data to credit officers, risk control and reporting users. It is an Oracle and Java based application that collates, processes, and de-normalizes credit risk information between different up-streams and down-streams through jobs and calculators and reports are run for all business days and month-ends.
